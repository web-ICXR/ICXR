Venue Information

ICXR 2026 will be held at Langham Place, Guangzhou, a modern conference hotel in Pazhou, Guangzhou. The venue is next to the Canton Fair Complex and offers convenient access to metro, airport, railway stations, and nearby business and sightseeing destinations.

Hotel Overview

Langham Place, Guangzhou features 488 guest rooms and suites, combining contemporary design, city views, and full-service conference hospitality. The hotel also provides multiple dining venues, spa and wellness facilities, and a broad range of indoor and outdoor event spaces suitable for international academic conferences.

Conference Facilities

Space Description
Function Space Approximately 5,000 square meters of flexible, high-tech event space, including pillar-free ballroom facilities.
Great Hall 2,800 square meters with an 11-meter ceiling and a 560-square-meter foyer; suitable for large plenary events and banquet functions.
Ballroom 760 square meters with a 500-square-meter foyer; can be divided into seven separate function rooms for parallel sessions.
Outdoor Terrace An outdoor event space on the 3rd floor, suitable for gatherings of up to 80 guests.
The Peak 200 square meters of indoor ceremony space for up to 100 guests, plus 800 square meters of outdoor area with views of Pazhou.

Dining and Wellness

  • Ming Court offers refined Cantonese cuisine and was selected in the Michelin Guide Guangzhou for five consecutive years from 2019 to 2023.
  • Alfresco is a semi-outdoor restaurant serving modern Italian cuisine.
  • The Open Kitchen is an all-day dining restaurant with international flavors and seasonal ingredients.
  • Portal and Sky Bar provide lounge and social spaces for informal meetings and evening gatherings.
  • Chuan Spa and the 24-hour Fitness Center offer wellness, massage, and workout facilities for guests.

Transportation Information

  • Metro Station: about 5 minutes on foot.
  • Canton Fair Complex: about 5–10 minutes on foot.
  • Pazhou Ferry Terminal: about 5 minutes by car.
  • Guangzhou East Railway Station: about 30 minutes by car.
  • Guangzhou South Railway Station: about 30 minutes by car.
  • Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport: about 40 minutes by car.

Nearby Highlights

The venue is located in Pazhou, one of Guangzhou’s major exhibition and innovation districts. In addition to the Canton Fair Complex, attendees may also find the following nearby attractions of interest:

  • Canton Tower – one of Guangzhou’s best-known landmarks and sightseeing destinations.
  • Historic Site of Ancient Whampoa Anchorage – a historic port area reflecting Guangzhou’s long trading history.
  • Party Pier – a lively waterfront district for dining, culture, and nightlife.
  • Tram Haizhu Line 1 – a convenient scenic tram route connecting several city landmarks.
