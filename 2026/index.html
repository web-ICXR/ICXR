2026 International Conference on Extended Reality (ICXR 2026)

The 2026 International Conference on Extended Reality (ICXR) is scheduled to be held in Guangdong Province, China, in November. ICXR is poised to be a landmark event, bringing together leading researchers, developers, industry leaders, and academics from around the globe in the field of extended reality technologies, including Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), and Mixed Reality (MR).
