2026 International Conference on Extended Reality (ICXR 2026)

The 2026 International Conference on Extended Reality (ICXR) is scheduled to be held in Guangdong Province, China, in November 12–13, 2026. ICXR is poised to be a landmark event, bringing together leading researchers, developers, industry leaders, and academics from around the globe in the field of extended reality technologies, including Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), and Mixed Reality (MR).

ICXR 2026 will feature a blend of keynote speeches, technical paper presentations, and panel discussions that explore the forefront of XR innovations, applications, and future trends. The conference will delve into a variety of compelling topics, such as metaverse development, advanced modeling techniques, perceptual interaction, graphics and image rendering, XR content creation, and digital twins.

We warmly invite all professionals, researchers, scholars, and enthusiasts from the XR community to converge in Qingdao for ICXR 2026. Engage with pioneering minds, discover groundbreaking advancements, and participate in a vibrant exchange of ideas that aim to shape the future of extended reality. Join us for an unforgettable experience in one of China's most beautiful coastal cities!
