Welcome to ICXR

The 3rd International Conference on Extended Reality (ICXR 2026)

The International Conference on Extended Reality (ICXR) is a premier academic conference dedicated to advancing research and innovation in Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), Mixed Reality (MR) and related technologies. Organized by the China Computer Federation (CCF) and hosted by its Virtual Reality and Visualization Technology Committee, ICXR 2026 continues its mission as part of the CCF's High-Quality International Academic Conference Incubation Program. The conference has been selected for inclusion in the CCF Recommended List of International Conferences.
