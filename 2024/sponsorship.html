Sponsorship Invitation

We are excited to invite you to become a sponsor for the 2024 International Conference on Extended Reality (ICXR 2024) taking place from November 14th to 17th in Xiamen, Fujian Province, China. As a sponsor, you will have the unique opportunity to engage with a global audience of industry leaders, researchers, and academics in the field of extended reality, including Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), and Mixed Reality (MR).
