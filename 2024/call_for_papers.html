Call for Papers

We are excited to announce the 2024 International Conference on Extended Reality (ICXR 2024) and invite the submission of original research papers. The conference will take place from November 14-17, 2024, in beautiful City, Xiamen.

Publication

All the full research papers will be published in the Proceedings of ICXR 2024 with Springer LNCS series (Indexing in EI, Scopus, DBLP, Google Scholar, etc).

Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • Virtual Reality
  • Augmented Reality
  • Mixed Reality
  • Extended Reality
  • Computer Graphics
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Computer Vision and 3D Vision
  • Multimedia
  • Image and Video Processing
  • Metaverse and Spatial Computing
  • Immersive Technologies
  • Applications of XR in Education, Medicine, and Entertainment
  • XR Hardware and Software
  • User Experience and Usability

Important Dates:

Paper Submission Deadline: October 7, 2024

Notification of Acceptance: October 22, 2024

Camera Ready Submission: October 30, 2024

Conference Dates: November 15-17, 2024

Submission Guidelines:

Authors are invited to submit original and unpublished research papers to ICXR 2024. All submissions will be peer-reviewed and evaluated based on originality, technical and research content, correctness, relevance to the conference, and readability.

Papers should be submitted in PDF format and should follow the standard conference template available on the conference website.

Paper Format

The maximum paper length is 15 pages in the LNCS one-column page format (including references and appendix).

Submission

Full papers in English containing original and unpublished results are solicited. The submission website is located at:
https://www.smartchair.org/ICXR-2024.

Double-blind Review

As the review process is double-blind, the authors must guarantee that the authors' names do not appear in the anonymous cover letter, manuscript, and research highlights.
