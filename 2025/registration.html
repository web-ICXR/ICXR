Conference Introduction

The International Conference on Extended Reality (ICXR) 2025 is set to take place on November 1-2 in the vibrant city of Qingdao, Shandong Province, China. Co-located with the prestigious ChinaVR 2025, ICXR promises to be a landmark event that brings together a global community of researchers, developers, industry leaders, and academics in the field of extended reality technologies, including Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), and Mixed Reality (MR).

ICXR 2025 will feature a blend of keynote speeches, technical paper presentations, and panel discussions that explore the forefront of XR innovations, applications, and future trends. The conference will delve into a variety of compelling topics, such as metaverse development, advanced modeling techniques, perceptual interaction, graphics and image rendering, XR content creation, and digital twins.

We warmly ivite all professionals, researchers, scholars, and enthusiasts from the XR community to converge in Qingdao for ICXR 2025. Engage with pioneering minds, discover groundbreaking advancements, and participate in a vibrant exchange of ideas that aim to shape the future of extended reality. Join us for an unforgettable experience in one of China's most beautiful coastal cities!
