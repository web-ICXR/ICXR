Tripo AI 3D Rendering Challenge

Fill in the Entry Form to join the Challenge!

(Note: By registering in challenge, you will receive 8000 time-limited credits to help you complete your creation.)

Theme

The work should be created around the theme of “Portal Pass”. The work should embody core concepts such as “transit” and “breaking through spatial boundaries”, and can be presented in different styles such as science fiction, fantasy, or reality.

Timeline

Submission Period: 2025/11/02 – 2025/11/23

Award Announcement: 2025/11/28

Rules

  • Strictly 7 seconds long.
  • Follow the template idea to fill in the scenes and characters.
  • At least one character and the “portal” on both sides.
  • Not specific to a human and not specific to walk.
  • Movement of the camera or the scene is allowed.
  • The main 3D models are recommended to be generated using Tripo Studio.
  • The rendering work are highly recommended using RAYSENGINE.
  • The work must be original and must not infringe upon any intellectual property rights of any third party.

Submission

1) Official Website Submission

  • A link to your uploaded video (upload on YouTube or Bilibili).

  • Publish the work on XiaoHongShu, Bilibili, DouYin, etc., @Tripo AI Official Account, and add the topic tag: #TripoRenderingChallengeS2.
  • Note: Social media submission is an additional display channel. The work must first be submitted through the official website; otherwise, it will be regarded as an invalid submission.
