Conference Introduction

The International Conference on Extended Reality (ICXR) 2025 is set to take place on November 1–2 in the vibrant city of Qingdao, Shandong Province, China. Co-located with ChinaVR 2025, ICXR brings together a global community of researchers, developers, industry leaders, and academics in VR/AR/MR.

ICXR 2025 features keynote speeches, technical paper presentations, and panel discussions exploring XR innovations and applications, including metaverse development, advanced modeling, perceptual interaction, graphics and image rendering, XR content creation, and digital twins.

We warmly invite XR professionals, researchers, scholars, and enthusiasts to Qingdao to engage, collaborate, and shape the future of extended reality.
