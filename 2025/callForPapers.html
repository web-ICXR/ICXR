Call for Papers

We are excited to announce the 2025 International Conference on Extended Reality (ICXR 2025) and invite the submission of original research papers. The conference will take place on November 1-2, 2025, in the beautiful city of Qingdao, Shandong.

Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • Virtual Reality
  • Augmented Reality
  • Mixed Reality
  • Extended Reality
  • Computer Graphics
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Computer Vision and 3D Vision
  • Multimedia
  • Image and Video Processing
  • Metaverse and Spatial Computing
  • Immersive Technologies
  • Applications of XR in Education, Medicine, and Entertainment
  • XR Hardware and Software
  • User Experience and Usability

Submission Guidelines:

Authors are invited to submit original and unpublished research papers to ICXR 2025. All submissions will be peer-reviewed and evaluated based on originality, technical and research content, correctness, relevance to the conference, and readability.

Papers should be submitted in PDF format and should follow the standard conference template available on the conference website.

Paper Format

The normal length of the main content is 15 pages (excluding references and appendices), but this is not a strict limit. Papers exceeding this length are acceptable and will be evaluated according to their overall contribution.

Submission

Full papers in English containing original and unpublished results are solicited. The submission website is located at:
https://easychair.org/conferences?conf=icxr2025.

Double-blind Review

As the review process is double-blind, the authors must guarantee that the authors' names do not appear in the anonymous cover letter, manuscript, and research highlights.

Publication

A number of selected papers presented at this year's ICXR will be recommended for submission and undergo a revision round before being published in prestigious journals, including Frontier of Computer Science (SCI), Computer & Graphics (SCI) and Virtual Reality & Intelligent Hardware (ESCI). All other full research papers will be published in the Proceedings of ICXR 2025 with Springer LNCS series (EI).
