Hotel Environment

The hotel covers an area of 100,000 square meters and features a unique Southeast Asian garden design. It offers a variety of banquet halls and is equipped with comprehensive multimedia audio-visual facilities.

The North Bay Hotel offers both above-ground and underground parking. Please provide your license plate number to the reception staff for 4 hours of free parking service.
