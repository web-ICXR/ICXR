Accommodation Information

ICXR 2025 will be held at the Haitian Financial Center Hotel (No. 29 Xianxialing Road, Laoshan District) in Qingdao City, Shandong Province. Hotel accommodation is 380 RMB/night. ICXR 2025 attendees who need to book a hotel are kindly requested to send the following information to icxr@ccf.org.cn.

    (1) Paper ID
     (2) Name
     (3) Phone number
     (4) Check-in time
     (5) Check-out time
     (6) Room Type (Queen/Twin). Note that we will try to satisfy your request, but this is not guaranteed.
     (7) Room quantity

Hotline:+86-532-87057777

Hotel Environment

The Hotel is an exceptional venue located in the vibrant Laoshan District of Qingdao. Boasting a contemporary design, the hotel spans a significant area and combines modern comfort with efficiency, making it an ideal choice for both business and leisure attendees.

Parking Services

The hotel offers both above-ground and underground parking. Please provide your license plate number to the reception staff for 3 hours of free parking service.
