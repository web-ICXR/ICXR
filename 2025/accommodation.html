Transportation Information

Qingdao Jiaodong International Airport (TAO):

• 56 km from the venue (55-minute taxi ride, costing approximately 185 RMB).

• 15 km from the venue (25-minute taxi ride, costing approximately 45 RMB).

Qingdao Railway Station:

• 16 km from the venue (28-minute taxi ride, costing approximately 48 RMB).

Qingdao West Railway Station:

• 62 km from the venue (80-minute taxi ride, costing approximately 210 RMB).

 

Public Transportation Route

Qingdao Jiaodong International Airport (TAO):

• Take Metro Line 8 (towards Qingdao North Railway Station), transfer to Metro Line 3 (towards Qingdao Railway Station) at Qingdao North Station, transfer to Metro Line 2 (towards Sichuan Road) at Licun Station, transfer to Blue Valley Express (towards Qiangu Mountain) at Miaoling Road Station, get off at Huizhan Center Station Exit B, and walk about 900 meters to reach the Haitian Financial Center Hotel.

Qingdao North Railway Station:

• Take Metro Line 3 (towards Qingdao Railway Station), transfer to Metro Line 2 (towards Sichuan Road) at Lijia Village Station, transfer to Blue Valley Express (towards Qiangu Mountain) at Miaoling Road Station, get off at Huizhan Center Station Exit B, and walk about 900 meters to reach the Haitian Financial Center Hotel.

Qingdao Railway Station:

• Take Metro Line 3 (towards Qingdao North Station), transfer to Metro Line 2 (towards Licun Park) at May Fourth Square Station, transfer to Blue Valley Express (towards Qiangu Mountain) at Miaoling Road Station, get off at Huizhan Center Station Exit B, and walk about 900 meters to reach the Haitian Financial Center Hotel.

Qingdao West Railway Station:

• The journey requires multiple transfers and takes about 3 hours, therefore not recommended.

Transportation Guide from Yantai to Qingdao

The earliest departure time from Yantai to Qingdao is 06:20 and the latest departure time is 21:30. On average, around 45 various types of high-speed C, G, D trains travel from Yantai to Qingdao per day between the times of 06:20 and 21:30 and the shortest train ride from Yantai to Qingdao takes about 1 hour.

Kindly notice:

China train tickets are available for purchase up to 15 days in advance. During peak travel periods, tickets can sell out quickly. To ensure a smooth journey, it's best to plan ahead and book your tickets as early as possible.
